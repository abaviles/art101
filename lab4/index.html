Task 1: Everyday Task

Making Eggo Waffles:

1. Buy Eggo waffles, syrup and butter from store.
2. Take 2 Eggo waffles out of packaging.
3. Put Eggos in toaster.
4. Wait for them to toast.
5. Put them on a plate.
6. Add butter and syrup.

Task 2: Computer Game Pseudocode

Creating Rock Paper Scissors:

1. Have player input Rock, Paper or Scissors.
2. Have computer make their move.
3. Compare responses between Player and Computer.
4. Create win or loss condition.
5. Repeat
